S. B. 34 CHAPTER 12

AN ACT TO AMEND CHAPTER 465 OF THE SESSION LAWS OF 1949
TO AUTHORIZE THE BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS OF
ROWAN COUNTY IN ITS DISCRETION TO ADD THE DUTIES
AND POWERS OF COUNTY TAX SUPERVISOR TO THOSE NOW
BEING PERFORMED BY THE COUNTY TAX COLLECTOR.

The General Assembly of North Carolina do enact:

Section 1. Section 1 of Chapter 465 of the Session Laws of 1949 is
hereby amended by rewriting Section 4 thereof to read as follows: “Sec. 4.
The Board of County Commissioners of Rowan County may, in its discre-
tion, add the duties and powers of County Tax Supervisor to those now
being performed by the County Auditor or by the County Tax Collector
and, in such event, may pay such County Auditor or County Tax Collector
such additional compensation for such services as, in its discretion, it may
deem appropriate.”

Sec. 2. All laws and clauses of laws in conflict with this Act are here-
by repealed.

Sec. 3. This Act shall be in full force and effect from and after its rati-
fication.

In the General Assembly read three times and ratified, this the 4th
day of February, 1955.
